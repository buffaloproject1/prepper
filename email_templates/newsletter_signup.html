Newsletter Field Guide
A half-remembered quote that goes, "Sit at my table and eat my food, you will know who I am." has always rung true for us. We believe food represents everything good about the world. A meal creates an atmosphere for laughter, conversations, debates, and just simple humanity. It's something we have always needed but now more than ever.

It has always been our passion to give everyone the tools and knowledge to be able to cook and bring back those conversations. Along the way, if we can sway someone to cook a little more organic and a bit more sustainable then we help create an impact that makes everyone's life a little better.

Our ideas are big and far-reaching making us excited and scared like the Apollo astronauts. Though it is our passion to help move cooking forward.

There is no expiration for the gratitude we have, to you, for the amount of hope you have given us by coming along on for the ride. We know there is no shortage of recipes and food blogs out there, so we are always challenge ourselves to make Knife & Fish your first choice.

Consider this email your field guide on what to expect and where to go.
